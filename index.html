OUR SERVICES

Comprehensive Corporate Solutions

We provide a wide range of corporate secretarial services to help your business meet regulatory requirements and operate efficiently.

Provision of Board Toolkit

A readily available logistics compiled by us to assist companies and their boards with their compliance duties. This has been categorized into:

Statutory Registrations

CSTS understands the stress associated with legitimate business registration and procedures, therefore our company offers to minimize the stress of individuals, companies, and institutions by rendering services of RGD, GIPC, GRA, on their behalf.

Process Server

We act as a process agent for our clients who may require this service, especially for clients who desire to register External Companies in Ghana.

Registered Box

CSTS services assist firms and business entities in obtaining a legal address so that they can receive official documents that are critical to their operations.

Consultancy Services

At CSTS, we take pride in having top professional experts who provide expert guidance in areas such as management, corporate governance, business law, and many more specialized subjects. CSTS has undertaken over 20 consulting projects in collaboration with a small number of legal-technology service providers, including Addens Technology Limited. Among our clientele are government, donor, and private sector clients in Ghana.

Company Secretarial Services

Our Company’s Secretarial Services is intended to help companies, institutions and other business entities meet their standard statutory compliance needs, assist clients to manage and mitigate risks of corporate non-compliance and innovative and support you (clients) with your administrative burdens.

Virtual Office

Our virtual office services allow clients to work from anywhere using a variety of internet-based business operations. Global adventurers who are tired of dealing with delayed mail, travel-heavy professionals who can’t wait to obtain essential documents on the return trip, or simply anyone who want to get rid of the clutter that comes with daily mail will benefit from CSTS’s Virtual Office service. We’ll assist you in establishing and maintaining a presence in a coveted area.
